Our Policies

Financing Policy

Flexible Payment Options
Blue Wave Sanitation offers flexible financing options to make our products accessible to individuals and communities:

  • Installment Plans: Customers can opt for monthly payment plans with zero or low interest for qualifying purchases.
  • Partnered Financial Institutions: We collaborate with trusted financial institutions to provide affordable loan options.
  • Eligibility: Financing is available for orders exceeding $500. Customers may need to provide proof of income or creditworthiness.

Application Process
- Submit a financing application through our website or authorized dealers.
- Applications are reviewed within 1-2 business days.

Terms and Conditions
- Financing agreements are subject to approval.
- Early repayments are allowed without penalties.
- Late payments may incur additional fees.

Water Quality Standards

All our products adhere to the highest international standards to ensure clean and safe water for communities around the world.

Community Engagement

We actively collaborate with local communities, offering educational programs and resources to promote water conservation and sanitation awareness.

Customer Satisfaction

Our policies ensure transparency and prioritize customer needs, delivering high-quality products and exceptional service.
